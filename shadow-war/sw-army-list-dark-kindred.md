**Dark Millenium: Shadow War**

Army List Dark Kindred
======================

## Army Special Rules

The following rules apply to all models in a Dark Kindred army.

### Prey on the Weak

When attacking an enemy model within assault range that is hurt the attacking Dark Kindred model gains +1 to their skill roll. When attacking models that are wounded this is increased to +2.

### Driven by Pain

When a model is hurt it does not suffer the -1 modifier to their movement, skill, or defence rolls.

## Named Special Rules

The following rules are given by name in unit profiles where they apply.

### Arena Champion

If this model has the Combat Drugs special rule they may roll 2 D12s and choose the result they wish, if both dice roll the same result they may re-roll one until it has rolled a different result.

### Combat Drugs

Models with this rule are able to boost their skills in battle. At the start of the models first activation, after it has been placed on the table, determine which effect the combat drugs will have by rolling a D12 and consulting the following table.

| D12 Result | Effect                                          |
| :--------: | :---------------------------------------------- |
| 1-3        | The model gains the Fast special rule           |
| 4-6        | The model gains +1 defence and +1 HP            |
| 7-9        | The model gains +1 skill and +1 strength        |
| 10-12      | The model gains +4 defence against free attacks |

The effect of the combat drugs should be noted on your army list.

### Lightning Reactions

Models with this rule gain +2 to their skill rolls when making free attacks against models that leave their assault range.

### Nightmare Grenades

Models which are hit with this weapon, for every defence roll they must make they must also make a command roll, for each roll they fail they take one suppression token up to their maximum.

### Shadow Hunter

Attacks against this model that have an obscured line of sight suffer a -2 to their skill roll.

### Soul Drain

Any time a model is killed within 6 inches of this model make a command roll, on a successful roll this model regains 1 lost hit point. This rule cannot take the model above it's starting hit points.

A Dark Kindred Warrior Leader is equipped with a Plasma Grenade and one weapon from each of the following options:
